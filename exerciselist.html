Scrambled Eggs

eggs are one of my favourite food . Here is a recipe of how to make a delicious scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Methods

  1. Melt butter in afrying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add eggs and butter
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan center every 20 seconds (as is you are making omellette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove them from the heat (it will continue to cook from the plate untill it is served )
