ORDERED LIST

  1. Chop potatoes into quaters
  2. Simmer into a salty water 15-20 minutes until tender
  3. Heat milk, butter and nutmeg
  4. Drain potatoes and mash
  5. Mix in the milk mixture

UNODERED LIST

  • 1 kg edward potatoes
  • 100 ml of milk
  • 50g salted water
  • Freshly grated nutmeg
  • salt and pepper to taste
